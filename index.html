TIG Design Solutions
Established in 2018, TIG Design and Solutions Center was founded by a diverse group of leaders with very strong corporate backgrounds, a common desire to pursue their entrepreneurial dreams, and a shared goal to give back to the local community. As a technology outsourcing company, TIG has expertise in technology and product development. We have extensive experience with various industry-recognized software development processes and across global, multicultural workplaces. TIG believes in practical team collaboration built on empowerment, trust and confidence in our teams’ ability to deliver. We leverage technology as an enabler to strike a balance between the things we love to do at work and quality personal time; keeping our people happy and our customers satisfied. TIG Design and Solutions Center is a Philippine-based company with headquarters in Cebu. We are duly registered with the Philippine Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), the Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) and the Mandaue City local government unit.​

Our Vision

is to be a valued partner of our customers and communities by providing technology and related services that enable them to succeed and exceed their expectations.

Our Mission

CUSTOMERS seek for value and long-term returns,
PARTNERS choose for their customers and
EMPLOYEES are proud of.
